Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

Description

The popular Japanese-inspired dish teriyaki chicken has juicy, delicate chicken covered in a savory-sweet glaze made with sake, sugar, mirin, and soy sauce. The rich, glossy sauce that gives the chicken its distinctive flavor can be brushed over after it has been perfectly pan-seared, roasted, or grilled. This dish is a tasty and filling supper that is frequently served with steamed rice and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (low-sodium preferred)
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon mirin (or white wine vinegar as a substitute)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • 1 tablespoon water (if using cornstarch)

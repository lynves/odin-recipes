Krabby Patty Formula

Description

The Krusty Krab, Bikini Bottom's underwater fast-food restaurant, serves the renowned Krabby Patty. A culinary landmark, the Krabby Patty is renowned for its mouthwatering flavor and flawlessly balanced composition. Its reputation stems from both its mouthwatering blend of fresh ingredients and the mystery surrounding its recipe, which is so well-kept that only a few people, including SpongeBob SquarePants, the passionate fry cook who produces them, are aware of the entire formula. Every Krabby patties is a masterpiece that entices consumers to return for more, whether it's the succulent, expertly grilled patties, the tender toasted bun, or the unique sauce.

Ingredients

  • The patty - 100% pure, fresh, and juicy crab or fish patty
  • Buns - Soft, perfectly toasted sesame seed buns or other local underwater buns (imagine a soft, pillowy texture).
  • Lettuce - Fresh, crispy lettuce, grown in Bikini Bottom
  • Pickles - Crunchy and tangy pickles, thinly sliced

  • The "secret sauce" that is used for the signature is, of course, the component that is still a mystery.
